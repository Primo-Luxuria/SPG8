Teacher FAQ

How do I add a new course?

Go to your dashboard and fill in the course details (ID, Name, CRN, Semester) along with the textbook. Click "Add Course".

How do I use an existing course?

Select a course from the dropdown under "Use Existing Course" and click "Add Course".

How do I assign textbooks to a course?

Select the textbooks and the course from their dropdowns, then click "Assign Selected Textbooks to Course".

How do I manage or edit questions?

Right-click on a question to edit, delete, or manage feedback using the context menu.

How do I import or parse QTI files?

To import QTI v1.2 files, go to your course dashboard and click "Import QTI". Upload your QTI ZIP file. QuizPress will automatically parse the file and extract all supported questions into your course or textbook.

How do I create a new quiz?

To create a new quiz, navigate to your dashboard, select "Add Quiz", and fill in the required details including title, questions, and settings. Save your quiz to publish it or keep it as a draft for later editing.

Can I reuse questions across different courses?

Yes, you can publish questions to your textbook library and then add them to any course that uses that textbook. Go to the Publisher section to manage shared question pools.

How do I import questions from a QTI file?

Click on "Import QTI" in your course dashboard, upload your QTI 1.2 ZIP file, and QuizPress will parse and add the questions automatically.

Is there a way to provide feedback to quiz takers?

Yes, you can enable feedback per question by editing a quiz and toggling the feedback option. Students will see correct answers and comments after submission.

Publisher FAQ

How do I add a textbook?

Use the textbook form to enter the Title, Author, Version, ISBN, and Link, then click "Add Textbook".

Where do I see my added textbooks?

They will show up in the textbook list below the form. If not, try refreshing the page.

How do I manage questions or feedback?

Right-click on a question to bring up the context menu for editing or managing feedback.
